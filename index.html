How to use these prompts

Each prompt is a ready-to-use starting point — not a magic button. The best results come when you treat them as a thinking partner: paste the prompt, fill in the context specific to your product and situation, then iterate on the output.

Step 1
Find a prompt
Browse by category or search by keyword to find a prompt that matches what you're working on.
Step 2
Copy & paste
Click any prompt card to open it, then hit "Copy Prompt" to copy it to your clipboard.
Step 3
Add your context
Paste into your AI tool of choice (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.) and replace any placeholders with specifics from your product, team, or situation.
Step 4
Iterate
Treat the first response as a draft. Ask follow-up questions, push back on weak reasoning, and refine until the output is actually useful.
